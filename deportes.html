Sports play a very important role in German society and culture. From an early age, many people participate in sports activities for fun or competition.

Football is the most popular sport, followed by handball, athletics, and winter sports.

Sports help promote health, teamwork, and discipline in Germany.
Football is the most popular sport. The Bundesliga is one of the best leagues in the world and Germany has won several World Cups. Handball is also very popular. Germany competes internationally in Olympic Games and World Championships.
Ice hockey has a long tradition and is played in professional leagues. Athletics is another strong sport where Germany has achieved many international medals.
